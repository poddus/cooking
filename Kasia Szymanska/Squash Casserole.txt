Squash Casserole

About 9 large fresh squash or 1 big can (sliced) squash

3    -  bell peppers
2    -  large onions
2    -  small jalapeno peppers (chopped fine)
1    -  large can Cream of Chicken soup
1    -  large container sour cream
 
Mix well and cook on the stove until ½ way done.
Remove from stove and let cool.
Add 4 eggs.
Mix well.
Top with Pepperidge Farm Stuffing mix (sage) and
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes.
 
Feeds 25
   
Note:  Cut recipe in half for a 9 x 13 pan
