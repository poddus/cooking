Yum Yum Bars

½ cup butter or margarine
1 c sugar 
1 egg  and 1 egg yolk
1 tsp vanilla
1 ½ cup flour
½ tsp salt 
1 tsp baking powder
1 egg white
1 cup light brown sugar (don’t pack)
1 cup pecans

Mix first seven ingredients. Put in greased 9x11 pan. Beat egg white until stiff. Fold in brown sugar and pecans and spread on batter
Bake 300  30-35 min
