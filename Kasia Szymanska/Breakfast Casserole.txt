BREAKFAST CASSEROLE #1
10 slices bread cubed
1.5# sausage
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
6 eggs
2 cups milk

Brown and drain sausage.  Place bread in the bottom of a greased 9 X 13 pan.  Layer sausage.  Layer cheese.  Combine eggs, milk, salt, and pepper and beat well.  Pour over sausage mixture.  Cover & refrigerate overnight.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 - 45 minutes.


CRACKER BARREL BREAKFAST CASSEROLE

2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 can cream of mushroom (or chicken)
1 cup sour cream
1 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 c. butter melted
1/4 tsp ea of salt and pepper
32 oz frozen hash brown potatoes shredded

1 cup crushed corn falkes and butter for top (optional)

Mix all ingredients except potatoes.  Fold in potatoes.  Bake in greased 9 X 13" pan for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
