Salmoriglio Sauce


2 tablespoons thyme leaves (or one dried)
1 Â½ tablespoons fresh lemon juice 
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
Salt
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

In food processor, combine the thyme leaves, lemon juice, mustard and salt.  Pulse for 1 minute.  Add the butter and process until completely smooth.  With the machine on, add the olive oil in a thin, constant stream until fully incorporated. Season the sauce with salt.

Best if made one day in advance to let the flavors mellow.
