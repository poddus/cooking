                          Poppy Seed Chicken
 
 
Cook:		6-8 boneless chicken breasts/cut into little pieces

Grease(Pam):	9"x13" casserole dish and put chicken into it

Mix:		2 cans cream of chicken soup
		1 cup sour cream
		Pour over chicken

Crush:		1 roll Ritz Crackers(35).  Combine with 2 T. poppy seeds and 11/2 sticks margarine and pour over top.  

Bake:		30-40 minutes at 350 degrees until bubbly.  Serves 6-8 people.
