Iza’s Pasta Salad

1 bunch of Cilantro
½ of an 8 oz jar of sun dried tomatoes in oil
1 8 oz chunk of feta cheese
1 16-oz box Barilla enriched pasta, spirals

Boil pasta in salted water until done.  Drain.  Keep in pot but remove from heat.

Finely chop sun dried tomatoes and add to pasta along with some of the oil.  Take fork to finely shred feta cheese and add to pasta.

Remove all cilantro leave from stems and finely chop.  Add to pasta.

Toss all together and serve.
