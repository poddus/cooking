Hot Reuben Spread
3¼ cups

Slow Cooker 1- 2 1/2 -quart
Prep Time: 10 min.
Cook Time: low 1 to 1 ½ hours
Hold Time: low up to 4 hours

1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
2 packages (2 1/2oz each) thinly sliced corned beef, chopped
1 ½ cups shredded Swiss cheese (6oz)
¾ cup drained sauerkraut
½ cup Thousand Island dressing
Pretzel, crackers or cocktail rye bread slices, for serving, if desired

Spread inside of 1-2 ½ -quart slow cooker with cooking spray.
2. Mix all ingredients except crackers; spoon into cooker.
Cover and cook on low heat setting 1 to 1 ½ hours or until cheese is melted. Stir until cheese is smooth.
Scrape down side of cooker with rubber spatula to help prevent edge of spread from scorching. Turn to low heat setting. 
Serve with crackers. Spread will hold up to 4 hours. 

1 Tablespoon: 40 calories, Diet exchange: 1 fat
