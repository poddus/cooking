
Romano Potatoes
2 lb red potatoes, unpeeled
3 green onions, chopped
1stick of butter
½ cup milk
2 tsp minced garlic
2 tsp salt
¼ cup grated Romano cheese
1 tsp. black pepper
2 T. reserved
½ tsp. white pepper
½ shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 tsp. paprika
½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Boil potatoes and cool in refrigerator. Once cool, cut potatoes into quarters and place in a large bowl. Heat butter, garlic and onion; pour over potatoes. Add cheese, milk, salt, pepper, and paprika. Mix ingredients together in a bowl, using hands and leaving large chunks of potatoes. Bake in 9x13-inch pan for 30 to 40 minutes at 350º.
