Holiday Salsa- the Cranberry Sauce

1 lb bag fresh cranberries
1 lg. red apple (delicious if available)
1 lg orange, rind included
2 cups sugar
2 jalapenos, seeded and minced

Seed apple and orange and cut into chunks. Wash cranberries.

Place cranberries, apple and orange in food processor and chop together. Stir in sugar and jalapeno. Mix very well. Prepare at least 48 hrs before serving. This salsa improves with age and freezes well. It makes welcome gifts.

Makes 3 cups
