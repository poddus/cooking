Curried Red Lentil and Swiss Chard Stew with Garbanzo Beans


2 tablespoons olive oil
l large onion, thinly sliced
5 teaspoons curry powder
¼ tsp cayenne pepper
3 14-ounce cans vegetable broth (I used 2 32-oz boxes)
l large bunch or 2 small bunches Swiss chard, tough stalks removed, coarsely chopped (about 12 cups) (I used one large bag of baby spinach)
1 pound red lentils (about 2 ¼ cups)  (I used the only lentils I could find, and they weren’t red)
1 15-ounce can garbanzo beans drained
Plain yogurt


Heat oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-high heat.  Add onion; sauté’ until golden, about 13 minutes.  Mix in curry and cayenne.  Add broth and chard.  Increase heat; bring to boil.  And lentils and garbanzos; reduce heat to medium.  Cover; simmer until lentils are tender, stirring twice, about 10 minutes.  Divide stew among bowls.  Top with yogurt.

6 servings
