Mediterranean Shrimp Soup

2 cans (14 ½ oz) chicken broth
1 can (14 ½ oz) whole tomatoes, undrained, coarsely chopped
1-8 oz tomato sauce
1 medium onion chopped
½ med green pepper, chopped
½ cup orange juice
½ cup dry white wine
2 ½ oz jar sliced mushrooms
¼ c. olives sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp dried basil leaves
2 bay leaves
¼ tsp fennel seed, crushed
1/8 tsp black pepper
1# medium shrimp, peeled


Put all ingredients into crock-pot except shrimp.  Cook on LOW 4-4 ½ hours until vegetables are crisp tender.  Stir in shrimp.  Cook 15-30 minutes more until shrimp are opaque.
