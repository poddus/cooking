Veggie Marinade


1 16-ounce can small green peas, drained
1 16-ouce can French-stle green beans, drained
1 11-ounce can shoe peg white corn, drained
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2/3 cup finely chopped celery
3 tablespoon chopped pimentos
2/3 cup sugar
½ cup vegetable oil
½ cup white wine vinegar
½ tsp white pepper

Combine vegetables.  In a saucepan, heat sugar, oil and wine vinegar until sugar dissolves.  Pour over vegetables and stir.  Chill overnight.
