Cranberry Punch  4 Cups of Cranberry Cocktail Juice 4 Cups of Pineapple Juice 1 1/2 Cups of sugar 1 tsp. of Almond Extract 2 Quarts of Ginger Ale, Chilled  Mix together the cranberry juice, pineapple juice, sugar, and almond extract. Chill. When ready to serve, combine the cranberry mixture with the ginger ale.  (I would love to try with orange extract!)
