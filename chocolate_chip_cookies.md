Chocolate Chip Cookies
======================

basic tollhouse cookie recipe, but with mostly mass units and adjusted for german standard grocery units. makes about 10 large cookies

Ingredients
-----------
* 125 g butter
* 175 g white sugar
* 10 g molasses
* 1 vanilla bean (ca. 5 g)
* 1 egg
* 175 g flour (type 405)
* 5 g baking powder
* 3 g salt
* 100 g chocolate chunks

Preparation
-----------
* preheat oven to 200Â°C
* combine ingredients
* scoop dough onto baking sheet (approx. ice cream scoop size)
* bake 15-18 minutes or until golden brown on the edges
