Chocolate Chip Cookies
======================

basic tollhouse cookie recipe, but pimped a bit and with mostly mass units and adjusted for german standard grocery units. makes about 10-15 large cookies

Ingredients
-----------
* 225 g butter
* 350 g white sugar
* 20 g molasses (optionally white and brown sugar half and half)
* 2 eggs
* 350 g flour (type 405)
* 15 g baking powder
* 200 g chocolate chunks (block chocolate)
* 5 g salt
* 30 ml rum, gold or dark
* 2 g ground vanilla bean

Preparation
-----------
* preheat oven to 180-200 Â°C (depends on the oven)
* brown the butter golden, set aside to cool
* combine ingredients
* scoop dough onto baking sheet (approx. ice cream scoop size)
* bake on the middle rack for 15-18 minutes or until golden brown on the edges
