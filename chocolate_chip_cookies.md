Chocolate Chip Cookies
======================

basic tollhouse cookie recipe, but with mostly mass units and adjusted for german standard grocery units.

Ingredients
-----------
* 250 g butter
* 350 g white sugar
* 20 g molasses
* 10 g vanilla bean (about 2 beans)
* 2 eggs
* 350 g flour (405)
* 10 g baking powder
* 5 g salt
* 200 g block chocolate

Preparation
-----------
* preheat oven to 200Â°C
* combine ingredients
* scoop dough onto baking sheet (approx. ice cream scoop size)
* bake 15-18 minutes or until golden brown on the edges
