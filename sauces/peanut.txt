Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
2 tsp. lime zest
1/4 cup lime juice
3 garlic cloves
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
1 tblsp. snipped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, lime zest and juice. Using a garlic press, press garlic into soy sauce mixture. Mix well. Reserve 1/3 cup soy sauce mixture for Peanut Dipping Sauce. Prepare Peanut Dipping sauce by adding peanut butter and cilantro to reserved 1/3 cup soy sauce mixture. Whisk until smooth.
