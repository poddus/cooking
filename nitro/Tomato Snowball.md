Tomato Snowball
==========================

Ingredients
-----------

* 400 ml white tomato stock
* 100 ml heavy cream
* 6 sheets of gelatin
* 1 × balloon
* salt, pepper

Preparation
-----------

* bring stock to a boil and dissolve the steeped gelatin in it.
* mix with the remaining stock and the heavy cream.
* Pour through a sieve into a 0.5 l Whipper, screw on 1 N2O Charger and shake vigorously.
* Cool in the refrigerator for 5 hours.
* Pour the snow into an air balloon and let the air out of the balloon until only the snow remains inside. 
* Close and knot the balloon and freeze at -18 °C for at least 1 hour.
