ginger beer
	0.5 g yeast
	syrup
		120 g sugar
		40 g molasses
		120 ml water
		100 g ginger
	50 ml lemon juice
	1.5 l water

* First, make the ginger syrup by combining the ingredients in a saucepan and heating until sugar dissolves. Remove from the heat, cover and allow to steep for 1 hour.
* Pour the syrup through a fine mesh strainer set over a bowl, pressing down to get all of the juice out of the mixture.
* Add remaining 1.5 l of cold water to syrup. the resulting mixture should have a temperature of about 20Â°C before adding the yeast and lemon juice.
